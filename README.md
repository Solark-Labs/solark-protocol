# 🏛️ SOLARK Protocol
**The Digital Handshake for Vehicle Ownership Transfer**

SOLARK (derived from the Sarala-Gokul lineage) is a high-fidelity middleware protocol designed to eliminate the "Trust Gap" in the Indian pre-owned vehicle market.

## 🚀 The Problem
Currently, selling a vehicle is digital, but legal transfer is broken. Sellers remain liable for accidents and fines long after handing over the keys because the RTO process is disconnected from the payment.

## 🛠️ The SOLARK Solution
SOLARK synchronizes the financial transaction with the legal RTO transfer through a 6-stage "Sync-Lock" engine:

1. **Baseline Proof:** Seller anchors the initial vehicle condition to the contract.
2. **10% Token Escrow:** Buyer commits a token amount to trigger the RTO NOC process.
3. **RTO API Handshake:** Direct communication with Vahan nodes to initiate Form 28/29 transfer.
4. **AI Handover Validation:** Middleman AI compares live handover photos against baseline data.
5. **Final Settlement:** Buyer clears the 90% balance once the condition is verified.
6. **Title Release:** Seller acknowledges final payout to unlock the Digital RC for the Buyer.
